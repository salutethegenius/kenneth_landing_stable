# 🌊 My Philosophy

Welcome, friend! In the Bahamas, we say, "The sea teaches patience, and the sun teaches joy." My philosophy is shaped by the islands—where every breeze brings a new idea, and every wave reminds us to keep moving forward.

## Rooted in Bahamian Culture
I believe wisdom grows best in good soil. For me, that soil is Bahamian: vibrant, resilient, and full of stories. From the straw markets to the regattas, I find inspiration in our people, our history, and our way of life.

## Curiosity is Key
Like a conch diver searching the blue, I’m always looking deeper. Curiosity drives my writing, my research, and my daily life. I ask questions, challenge old ideas, and welcome new perspectives—especially those that come with a taste of sky juice or a Junkanoo beat.

## Learning is a Lifelong Regatta
Every day is a chance to learn—whether from books, from neighbors, or from the sea itself. I believe in sharing what I discover, so we can all sail a little further together.

## Joy in the Journey
Happiness isn’t just a destination; it’s the journey. I try to find joy in small things: a good story, a fresh breeze, a laugh with friends. That’s the Bahamian way.

Thanks for stopping by. May your own journey be filled with sunshine, wisdom, and a little island magic.
