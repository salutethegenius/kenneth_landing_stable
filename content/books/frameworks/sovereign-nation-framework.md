    <div class="section">
        <h2>🔧 2. Sovereign Systems Stack</h2>
        <p>Here are the 9 core systems every sovereign nation or project must either own or rebuild:</p>

        <h3>1. Education Infrastructure</h3>
        <ul>
            <li>Open-source curricula grounded in local history + global skills</li>
            <li>Digital literacy from age 7</li>
            <li>Youth sovereign tech builder programs</li>
        </ul>

        <h3>2. Digital Identity + Data Ownership</h3>
        <ul>
            <li>Citizen-controlled identity systems (self-sovereign ID)</li>
            <li>National data centers (encrypted and locally hosted)</li>
            <li>Data portability and ownership protections</li>
        </ul>

        <h3>3. Financial Infrastructure</h3>
        <ul>
            <li>National e-wallets and cross-border crypto rails</li>
            <li>Central sovereign wealth fund with transparent royalty tracking</li>
            <li>Local banking APIs and digital microloans</li>
        </ul>

        <h3>4. Media and Narrative Control</h3>
        <ul>
            <li>Independent public media platforms (news, culture, entertainment)</li>
            <li>Digital storytelling academies</li>
            <li>Protection against foreign media manipulation</li>
        </ul>

        <h3>5. Agriculture and Food Resilience</h3>
        <ul>
            <li>Micro-farming and hydroponics network</li>
            <li>National seed banks</li>
            <li>Agro-data cooperatives using drones + AI</li>
        </ul>

        <h3>6. Energy Sovereignty</h3>
        <ul>
            <li>Island microgrids</li>
            <li>Solar + ocean current integration</li>
            <li>Citizen energy shares or tokenized ownership</li>
        </ul>

        <h3>7. Civic Technology + Governance Tools</h3>
        <ul>
            <li>Public contract transparency dashboards</li>
            <li>Participatory budgeting platforms</li>
            <li>Decentralized local governance nodes (digital town halls)</li>
        </ul>

        <h3>8. Defense of Mind + Culture</h3>
        <ul>
            <li>Anti-disinformation task force</li>
            <li>Cultural protection programs for language, ritual, and heritage</li>
            <li>AI/VR-powered cultural museums for youth</li>
        </ul>

        <h3>9. Global Trade + Talent Exchange</h3>
        <ul>
            <li>Regional sovereign tech coalitions (e.g. Bahamas–Barbados Digital Pact)</li>
            <li>Diaspora return pipelines</li>
            <li>Tech and education export packages</li>
        </ul>
    </div>
