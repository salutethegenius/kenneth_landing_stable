            <li><strong>Local empowerment with global reach</strong></li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="method-principle">
        <h3>⚖️ 2. Operate on Dual Philosophies</h3>
        <p>You see the world through a <strong>naturalist's realism</strong> and an <strong>idealist's imagination</strong>.</p>
        
        <ul>
            <li>Grounded in hard truth: money, systems, exploitation, realpolitik</li>
            <li>Yet open to spirit: belief, higher calling, legacy, culture, God-in-man</li>
        </ul>
        
        <blockquote>"It's not just business — it's structure with soul."</blockquote>
    </div>

    <div class="method-principle">
        <h3>🧭 3. Move Quiet, Build Loud</h3>
        <p>You don't announce change — you <strong>prototype it</strong>.<br>
        Your life is lived in <strong>execution cycles</strong>, not performative cycles.</p>
        
        <ul>
            <li>Education before protest</li>
            <li>Code before outrage</li>
            <li>Systems before slogans</li>
        </ul>
        
        <p>You know that a well-built platform speaks louder than a podium.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="method-principle">
        <h3>🧱 4. Decentralize the Power, Centralize the Vision</h3>
        <p>You're not a boss who hoards — you're a <strong>builder who multiplies</strong>.<br>
        You train others, clone systems, and make knowledge open — but your <strong>core thesis remains centralized</strong>.</p>
        
        <p>Your structure resembles:</p>
        <ul>
            <li>A sovereign tech OS</li>
            <li>A federated group of tools and minds</li>
            <li>A cultural protocol disguised as a company</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="method-principle">
        <h3>🌍 5. Always Serve the Underserved</h3>
        <p>You don't build for the elite — you <strong>build tools for the overlooked</strong>:</p>
        <ul>
            <li>Small businesses</li>
            <li>Schools</li>
            <li>Black youth</li>
            <li>Caribbean nations</li>
        </ul>
        
        <blockquote>"If my solution can't work in Freeport, it's not finished."</blockquote>
    </div>

    <div class="method-principle">
        <h3>🧬 6. Legacy Over Noise</h3>
        <p>You don't just want profits — you want your daughter, your team, your island to inherit <strong>a better operating system for life</strong>.</p>
        
        <ul>
            <li>This is why you log everything</li>
            <li>Why you structure IP like a vault</li>
            <li>Why you question everyone — including yourself</li>
        </ul>
        
        <blockquote>"I don't want to be remembered. I want what I built to outlive me."</blockquote>
