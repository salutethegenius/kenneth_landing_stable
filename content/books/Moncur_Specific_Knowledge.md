<div class="book-container">
    <div class="book-header">
        <h1 class="book-title">🎯 Find and Build Your Specific Knowledge</h1>
        <p class="book-subtitle">By Kenneth Moncur</p>
    </div>

    <div class="intro-section">
        <p>Let me tell you something that changed my life — and it'll change yours if you take it seriously:</p>
        <p><strong>Wealth, impact, and freedom don't come from doing what everyone else does.</strong><br>
        They come from tapping into your <strong>specific knowledge</strong> — that rare mix of what you know, what you've lived, and how you see the world.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="knowledge-section">
        <h3>🔍 What Is Specific Knowledge?</h3>
        <p>Specific knowledge is the thing <strong>only you can do like you do</strong>. It's not taught in school, and no one hands it to you.</p>
        
        <p>It's what happens when:</p>
        <ul>
            <li>You go deep into something that <em>feels like play to you but looks like work to others</em></li>
            <li>You combine your <strong>obsessions</strong>, <strong>culture</strong>, <strong>background</strong>, and <strong>vision</strong></li>
            <li>You learn and build in a way that <strong>can't be copied</strong></li>
        </ul>
        
        <blockquote>You want to be irreplaceable? This is how.</blockquote>
    </div>

    <div class="knowledge-section">
        <h3>🧭 How to Find It</h3>
        <p>Ask yourself these questions. Be real.</p>
        
        <ul>
            <li>What do I <em>obsess over</em> even when no one's watching?</li>
            <li>What do people always come to me for?</li>
            <li>What have I mastered <em>without</em> anyone teaching me?</li>
            <li>What could I talk about for hours and never get bored?</li>
            <li>What feels easy for me but hard for others?</li>
        </ul>
        
        <p>When you answer that — that's your starting point. That's your <strong>edge</strong>.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="knowledge-section">
        <h3>🔨 How to Build It</h3>
        <p>Here's what I did — and you can do the same in your own way:</p>
        
        <ol>
            <li><strong>Pick a problem</strong> that actually matters — to your people, your island, your circle.</li>
            <li><strong>Study it deeper than anyone around you.</strong> Books, podcasts, interviews, real-world reps.</li>
            <li><strong>Document the journey</strong> — build in public, teach while you learn.</li>
            <li><strong>Apply it.</strong> Solve real problems. Don't just talk — build tools, systems, products.</li>
            <li><strong>Repeat and refine</strong> — until it feels like second nature.</li>
        </ol>
    </div>

    <div class="knowledge-section">
        <h3>🧠 My Own Example</h3>
        <p>I didn't wait for someone to give me a seat at the table. I built my own system:</p>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>VerityOS</strong> for AI sovereignty</li>
            <li><strong>Minty</strong> to teach money control</li>
            <li><strong>Kemis Academy</strong> to open free education</li>
            <li>I mix AI, economics, design, and local insight in a way no foreigner could replicate</li>
        </ul>
        
        <p>That's <em>my</em> specific knowledge. Yours will be different — but just as powerful.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="knowledge-section">
        <h3>🚫 What Not To Do</h3>
        <ul>
            <li>Don't waste years being a generalist.</li>
            <li>Don't think your uniqueness isn't valuable.</li>
            <li>Don't wait for validation — <strong>build first, validate later.</strong></li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="knowledge-section">
        <h3>💡 What You Can Build With It</h3>
        <p>Once you know your specific knowledge, you can:</p>
        <ul>
            <li>Create digital products</li>
            <li>Build systems people depend on</li>
            <li>Lead with confidence</li>
            <li>Teach others, monetize, and scale</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="final-word">
        <h3>✊ Final Word</h3>
        <p><strong>Specific knowledge is your God-given fingerprint in business.</strong><br>
        Find it. Build it. Own it. Use it to free your family, your people, and your legacy.</p>
        <p>No excuses. Get obsessed. Build what only you can build.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="author-signature">
        –– Kenneth Moncur
    </div>
